Personal website of

Jon Robson

(jdlrobson)

I'm a Welsh/English/European open source web developer and writer living in San Francisco.
I work for the Wikimedia Foundation.
I built the mobile Wikipedia experience.

I like to build things that remove barriers for non-technical people to empower them to fulfil their dreams.
I am curious about anything that helps us retain and celebrate the beauty of different cultures.
I contribute to the web in the hope that I will leave it a better place for the children I'll never have.
I write to share ideas.
