Riot map

I (@rakugojon) was curious to see what the correlation was between the riots yesterday and deprived areas. Luckily two Young Rewired Staters (Priyesh Patel and Daniel Saul) who spent time in our office last week had done the hard work with their excellent mood map and imported government deprivation data into google fusion tables. All I had to do was filter this data to just show the areas seen as more deprived (I took areas with a score of over 50) and overlay it with the live London riots map compiled by James Cridland. To see the deprived areas, simply zoom in on a marker (signifying a riot). Dark areas nearby represent deprived areas that you can click on. Hope you find this interesting. Please help keep this map up to date by tweeting @jamescridland with verifiable source.
